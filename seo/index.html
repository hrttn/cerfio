Search Engine Optimization for busy agencies

Your agency is growing and some of your clients are asking for SEO on top of their website management and creation. The problem? You don’t have the time to invest in SEO nor the resources to hire someone full-time.

Why not use a White Label SEO?

What is white-label SEO?

The concept of white-label SEO is simple: we provide SEO services for your client acting as your agency. We do not mention Cerf.io at all; your client will never hear of this brand; for them, it’s all done through your agency.

  • You can now offer high-quality Search Engine Optimization services to your clients.
  • Your clients get what they need and have only one trusted source: you

Why use Cerf.io for your SEO?

  1. You can deliver SEO to your clients and keep them happy

  3. No need to pay for pricey SEO software (some start at €100/month)
  4. You can focus on what you do best
  5. You can stop at any time, no justification needed
  6. You can charge your client what you want (easy recurring revenue)
